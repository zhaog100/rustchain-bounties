## How to Record

1. Use a simple recording setup (phone, computer mic)
2. Add sea shanty-style instrumentation:
   - Acoustic guitar
   - Fiddle/strings
   - Drums
   - Accordion
3. Upload audio file + lyrics to rustchain-bounties
