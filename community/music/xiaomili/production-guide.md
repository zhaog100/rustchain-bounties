# RustChain Sea Shanty Production Guide

## Musical Structure
- **Key:** D minor (traditional sea shanty key)
- **Time Signature:** 6/8 (classic shanty rhythm)
- **Tempo:** 120 BPM
- **Style:** Call-and-response traditional sea shanty

## Instrumentation
### Minimal Setup (Voice Memo Quality):
- Lead vocals (call)
- Group vocals or echo effect (response)
- Optional: simple percussion (clapping, tapping)

### Full Production:
- Acoustic guitar (Dm, C, Bb, F progression)
- Bodhrán or frame drum (6/8 rhythm)
- Bass (root notes only)
- Harmonica (optional, D minor scale)
- Group vocals for chorus responses

## Recording Instructions
1. **Voice Recording:** Use any voice memo app, phone recorder, or DAW
2. **Quality:** Minimum 30 seconds, clear enough to understand lyrics
3. **Format:** MP3, WAV, or OGG
4. **Length:** Approximately 2 minutes (3 verses + choruses + outro)

## Audio Generation Alternative
If recording isn't possible, use text-to-speech with singing capabilities:
- Tools: Uberduck.ai, Voicemod, or similar TTS services
- Settings: Male voice, moderate pace, emphasize rhythm
- Post-process: Add reverb and slight echo for "group" effect
