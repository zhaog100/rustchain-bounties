# The Ballad of RustChain

## Verse 1
In the year of twenty-twenty-six, when devs began to flee,
Ethereum lost its builders, Solana lost its key.
But Scotty had a vision, of machines both old and rusted,
Proof-of-Antiquity was born, where vintage gear is trusted!

## Chorus
**So mine you old PowerMac, mine you rusty 486!**
